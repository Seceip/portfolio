About Me

I'm a Junior Full Stack Developer and a Software Development and Computer Science student. I enjoy working across the full stack, learning new technologies, and improving my problem-solving skills through hands-on projects.

I value clean code, good practices and continuous learning. In addition to development, I have experience with English translation and voice-related work, which helps me communicate effectively and work with international content and teams.

Outside of development, I enjoy exploring complex systems, including games, which has reinforced my interest in logic, optimization and how different systems interact.
